A Note about Our Staff

We pride ourselves in responding to and handling our customers' concerns immediately. This is due to our staff of professionals which include:


Mike Wilson - General Manager
Mike Wilson is an outstanding associate that has been with our Todd Haley & Associates since 2004. Mike is responsible for our customer base in South Carolina and Georgia territories. He also services national retail stores located in Central and South Georgia. Mike has a great deal of experience in the cabinet industry, from manufacturing to retail sales. Mike Wilson was the Thomasville Sales Representative of the year for MasterBrand Cabinets in 2008.


Cheryl Weaver - Sales Manager
Cheryl Weaver is an outstanding associate that has been with Todd Haley & Associates since 2005. Cheryl’s excels in servicing and selling national retail stores located in Atlanta and North Georgia and is the Sales Manager for our agency. Cheryl brings experience from a variety of areas including retail kitchen sales and kitchen design. Cheryl Weaver has been nominated for Rep of the Year many times and won Rep of the Year in 2010.


Paul Cunningham
Paul joined Todd Haley & Associates in September 2013. Paul brings 36 years of experience in the kitchen and bath industry. His experience includes 10 years working in the wholesale channel as Department Administrator and Sales Manager. Additionally, 4 years as Operations Manager for a custom design showroom and 18 years in retail design, sales and project management for custom, semi-custom and stock cabinetry. Paul’s vast experience is a valuable asset to our team. Paul was Rep of the Year 2016 and nominated as Rep of the year 2017. Paul is responsible for servicing and growing our Tennessee, Kentucky and West Virginia markets.


Joshua Wilson
Joshua Wilson has been a member of the Todd Haley & Associates team since May of 2011. Joshua’s work experience in sales and cabinet design have benefitted him and the team greatly since he joined. Joshua worked as a Cabinet Designer with Home Depot for over six years where he won numerous awards for customer service and sales. He also took the steps to open and manage a cabinet division for a local lumber supply for 2 years. Since Joshua has been with Todd Haley & associates, he has won numerous awards and has been nominated for Rep of the Year three times. We look forward to many more years and continued growth with Joshua!


Ami Harrison
Ami Harrison joined the Todd Haley & Associates team in March 2013. She has over 15 years of years of experience in the kitchen and bath industry and a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from UNC Charlotte. Her experience includes servicing a multitude of customers that range from individuals, builders, Home Centers, Kitchen and Bath dealers to fabricators. Ami is involved in servicing and expanding our reach in the eastern North Carolina and Virginia markets.


Angeline Hannachi
Angeline joined Todd Haley & associates in August 2013. Angeline’s kitchen design and project management experience is a great asset to our team. Prior to coming on board she worked at a large full service remodeling firm in Northern Virginia for 5 years where she helped launch their kitchen and bath design showroom. She also oversaw many beautiful full scale kitchen and bathroom renovations. Prior to transitioning into the kitchen industry, Angeline was a marketing and production designer for an industrial sign company which, interestingly enough, produced signage for the Home Depot. With literally a lifetime’s worth of design experience Angeline brings a wealth of knowledge, skill and experience to the team. Angeline will be responsible for growing and servicing our Northern Virginia and DC markets.


Thomas Clarke
Thomas Clarke CKD joined the Todd Haley & Associates team in May 2015. Tom was awarded his CKD after passing tests for it at KBIS in New Orleans in 2013. Having worked in residential construction and remodeling in different capacities since 1987, Tom began working as a kitchen & bath designer in 1999 for The Home Depot. After 6 years he accepted a position with a locally owned retailer which sold several MasterBrand lines. While with this firm Tom helped with the design of two showrooms and served as the lead designer responsible for mentoring new designers. Tom began private design consulting in 2009 and became involved with the local NKBA Chapter as a volunteer. Tom has served as VP of Academic Relations, VP of Professional Development and VP of Governmental Relations. Tom is responsible for servicing our customer base in Louisiana, Mississippi, Alabama, and the Florida Panhandle.


Judson G. Haley
Judd Haley joined Todd Haley & Associates in August 2016. Upon graduating from Mississippi State University, Judd started his cabinet career by accepting a position with Founders Kitchen & Bath in Alpharetta, GA. Here Judd was able to learn the kitchen and bath business from the dealer side. After gaining this experience Judd started with Todd Haley & Associates in a sales position servicing Georgia, South Carolina, and the Florida panhandle. After two years working with our National Retail Accounts across the South he was promoted to a position handling Independent and national accounts in North Carolina and relocated to Wilmington, NC.


Valerie Hurst, ASID, CKD
Valerie Hurst joined Todd Haley & Associates in March 2018. A degreed professional (Bachelor of Interior Design, Bassist College) in the interior design field Valerie has also attained NCIDQ, ASID, CKD and CID Virginia certifications. Valerie served as President of the Alaska Chapter of ASID, (2 terms). She has the distinction of winning numerous awards from coast to coast and was most recently selected as a finalist by HGTV to be featured in a reality television show based on her recently sold company “Kitchens by Valerie LLC.” Valerie has over 30 years of design experience in the commercial office and residential kitchen industry, most recently as the managing principal of her own boutique kitchen dealership in Alaska where she sold MasterBrand products. Valerie brings the pedigree, experience, and empathy most desired by designers and owners of today’s retail kitchen showrooms. She excels in space planning, design, mill-work details, finish selection, construction documents, project management and customer service. Valerie brings a fresh new approach to kitchen representation, one we feel certain is necessary in our ever-changing retail landscapes. Valerie is responsible for growing and servicing our North Carolina Charlotte and western North Carolina markets.


Ginny Edens
Ginny joined the Todd Haley and Associates team in February 2012. She began her career in the Kitchen and Bath industry in 1995, after earning a Bachelor of Science in Interior Design from Georgia Southern University. Her professional resume includes: Kitchen and Bath Specialist at a national home center, design and sales with independent kitchen and bath dealers, sales and measuring with a custom solid surface and stone fabricator, and Showroom Manager for a kitchen and bath dealer. She currently fills the role of Office Support/Inside Sales for Todd Haley and Associates.


Megan Elsey

She supports our sales team and dealers throughout our territory to help ensure the best customer service possible.

A Note From Todd

Hello! Thank you for visiting my web site. For the last 20 years, I have been involved in the kitchen industry as a partner in a kitchen dealership and as a sales rep working directly for a manufacturer. With this knowledge and experience, I started a multi-line agency in 1997 serving Georgia, Alabama, and the Florida Panhandle. In the past 16 years, I have represented several brands from MasterBrand Cabinets and serviced different channels of distribution. I am proud that I have consistently represented one industry leading manufacturer with portfolio of products that best meet my customer’s needs. Since 1997, we have expanded and have taken on more channels and brands for MasterBrand Cabinets and we have taken on more geography. We are now also responsible for servicing Mississippi, Kentucky, Tennessee, Louisiana, South Carolina, North Carolina, Virginia & West Virginia for certain channels of distribution and MasterBrand product offerings.

It is my belief that by helping you to achieve your business goals that we will succeed along with you. I focus on my customer's success and profitability, not just product! At Todd Haley & Associates, we can help you develop marketing and business plans as well as sales plans.

I would appreciate the opportunity to help you in any way. Give us a call!
